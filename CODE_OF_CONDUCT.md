# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

**Be respectful.** Disagreement is fine; personal attacks are not.

**Be constructive.** Focus on improving the project, not criticizing people.

**Be patient.** This is a side project maintained in spare time.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and may result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.
